1.Bahubali

Cast:Prabhas,Rana,Anushka,Tamanna,Ramya krishna

Director:S.S.Rajamouli

Music Director: M.M.Keeravani

Producer:shobu Yarlagadda

Story Summary:

A woman escapes from a cave under a waterfall with a baby but dies while praying to Lord Shiva to protect the child. A tribal couple rescues and raises the boy as Sivudu. As he grows up, he becomes strong and determined to climb the giant waterfall. At the top, Sivudu meets Avantika, a rebel warrior trying to free Princess Devasena from the cruel king Bhallaladeva. Sivudu takes on her mission, enters Mahishmati, and rescues Devasena. During the rescue, people recognize him as Baahubali. The royal commander Kattappa reveals that Sivudu is actually Mahendra Baahubali, the son of the heroic prince Amarendra Baahubali. He explains how Amarendra Baahubali was chosen as the future king but was later betrayed and killed. Shockingly, Kattappa admits that he was forced to kill Baahubali, setting up Sivudu’s destiny to seek justice.

Movie Key Points

  1. A story about brothers battling for the throne of the fictional Mahishmati kingdom.
  2. The strong and adventurous Shivudu climbs the massive waterfall near his village.
  3. who realizes Shivudu is the son of the late King Amarendra Baahubali.
  4. Kattappa begins narrating the story of Amarendra Baahubali's past.
  5. The film ends with the iconic question: "Why did Kattappa kill Baahubali?".

Box office Collection

  • Total Worldwide Gross: Approximately ₹600–650 crore.
  • It was the first South Indian film to gross over ₹300 crore.
  • The first South Indian film to net ₹100 crore in the Hindi market.
  • Became the highest-grossing film in India at the time of its release.
  • It performed strongly in overseas markets, with a significant opening in the US and Canada.
